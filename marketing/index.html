Pi Kappa Alpha

Mu Tau Chapter

Our chapter is located at the University of Northern Colorado in Greeley, Colorado. We are dedicated to promoting leadership, scholarship, and service among our members. Our core values include Integrity, Diversity, Loyalty, Responsibility, and Excellence, which are reflected in our commitment to academic achievement, personal growth, and community service. Our fraternity believes in the importance of building lifelong relationships among our members and supporting each other in all aspects of life. Through our various programs and initiatives, we aim to inspire our members to make a positive impact on the world and to uphold the highest standards of character and integrity. Overall, we strive to create a community of leaders who are committed to making a difference in our communities and in the world.

Fraternity life at the University of Northern Colorado offers a unique opportunity for students to develop lifelong friendships and leadership skills. Pi Kappa Alpha fraternity is one of the many organizations on campus that provides its members with a supportive community where they can grow both personally and professionally. Our fraternity hosts various social and philanthropic events throughout the year, allowing its members to engage with the broader campus community and make a positive impact on those around them. Being a member of Pi Kappa Alpha fraternity means being part of a brotherhood that values integrity, academic excellence, and service, and is committed to helping each member reach their full potential.

SLAG

Scholar

Being a scholar is important because it helps us expand our knowledge, develop critical thinking skills, and pursue our passions. It also demonstrates a commitment to excellence and sets us apart as individuals who are dedicated to personal growth and intellectual development.

Leader

We love to build our members leadership skills. Leadership is essential for success, as it involves inspiring and motivating others, communicating effectively, and having a clear vision for the future. Strong interpersonal skills and accountability are also crucial for effective leadership.

Athlete

Athletics promotes physical and mental health, while developing discipline, teamwork, and leadership skills. It requires dedication and hard work, and provides opportunities for personal growth and lifelong friendships.

Gentlemen

Being a gentleman involves embodying values such as respect, kindness, and accountability towards others, and striving to be the best version of oneself.
